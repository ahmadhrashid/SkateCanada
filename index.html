National Team

July 1, 2024 – June 30, 2025

The National Team comprises those athletes who meet the following criteria:

  • Top three in senior singles, pair, and ice dance disciplines at the 2024 Canadian National Skating Championships
  • Assigned to a 2024/2025 ISU Grand Prix event at the ISU Grand Prix Selection meeting
  • Competing in the 2024/2025 season
  • Skate Canada has the discretion to add athletes/teams during the 2023-2024 season
  • The Synchronized National Team comprises those teams finishing in the top three in the senior discipline at the 2024 Canadian National Skating Championships.
